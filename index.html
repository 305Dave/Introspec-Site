Why I Built This

I’ve always been drawn to the med spa and aesthetics industry — what it does for people’s confidence, and how these businesses deserve systems that match their level of care.

When I started looking at how these businesses actually operate, I kept seeing the same gap. Great services. Real demand. Leads slipping away because nobody could respond fast enough.

A potential client reaches out after hours, or between sessions, or while the owner is with a patient. By the time someone follows up — hours later, sometimes the next day — they’ve already booked somewhere else.

I built Introspec Agentic to close that gap. A fully managed AI system that captures every inquiry, responds in under 60 seconds, follows up automatically, and keeps the owner informed — so the spa never loses a lead to slow follow-up again. Done for you. Running 24/7. No staff required.

Built exclusively for med spas — every feature, every message, every automation designed specifically for this industry.

Founder, Introspec Agentic
